# Prompt Examples & Comparisons

## Bad Prompt
"man in city"

## Good Prompt
"cinematic portrait of man walking in rainy neon city, reflections, moody lighting, cyberpunk style"

---

## Before / After Optimization

### Before:
"robot fighting"

### After:
"cinematic robot battle in destroyed city, smoke, sparks, dramatic lighting, ultra realistic style"

---

## Optimization Tips

- Add lighting keywords
- Add camera angle (close-up, wide shot)
- Add mood (dark, cinematic, dreamy)
